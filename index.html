Introduction of ChatGPT

Topics that need to detail explanation in this project.

  • What technology the team choose?

    • Our team chose ChatGPT, a powerful artificial intelligence language model developed by OpenAI.

  • Providing introduction about the ChatGPT.

    • ChatGPT is an advanced generative AI system that can understand questions, process information, and create human-like responses. It can write essays, explain concepts, generate ideas, translate languages, summarize information, and assist with problem-solving.

  • Why the CatGPT Technology is important?

We chose ChatGPT because it is one of the most influential and widely used technologies today, especially in education. It helps students study more effectively, supports teachers in creating materials, and improves access to learning for people who may not have tutors or extra resources. ChatGPT matters because it is changing how people learn, communicate, and work.

Application of the Technology

Example 1: ChatGPT in Education Students use ChatGPT to understand difficult topics, summarize readings, and improve writing. Teachers use it to create lesson plans, quizzes, and study materials. Challenge: Students may misuse it to do assignments for them, raising concerns about cheating and reduced learning. Why it matters: It affects academic honesty and the overall quality of education.
Example 2: ChatGPT in Customer Service Companies use ChatGPT-powered chatbots to answer questions, solve problems, and provide 24/7 support. This improves response time and reduces workload for human workers. Challenge: ChatGPT may misunderstand questions or give wrong information. Why it matters: Miscommunication can frustrate customers and damage a companys reputation.

Critical Discussion about ChatGPT

  • Social Implications, Harms, and Risks:
    • ChatGPT can spread misinformation if its answers are incorrect.
    Raises concerns about academic honesty (students relying on AI instead of learning).
    This can worsen job displacement fears in fields like customer service and writing.
    Risks of bias in responses, which may reflect unfair or harmful patterns from its training data.
  • Ethical Lenses and Interpretation:
    • Utilitarianism (Greatest Good for the Greatest Number)
    ChatGPT as beneficial because it increases access to education, speeds up work, and provides support to millions.
    on rules and responsibilities: students must use ChatGPT ethically, companies must avoid deception, and AI developers must prioritize safety.
  • Virtue Ethics (Character and Intent)
    • Emphasize whether users show integrity, honesty, and responsibility when using ChatGPT.
    Using ChatGPT to cheat forms bad habits, while using it to learn can build positive character.
    Social Justice Lens
